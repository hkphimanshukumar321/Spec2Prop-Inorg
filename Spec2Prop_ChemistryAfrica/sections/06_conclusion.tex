\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

In this work, we introduced Spec2Prop-InorgBench, a comprehensive Raman--XRD benchmark designed for materials informatics. By open-sourcing the runtime preprocessing pipeline and providing chemistry-aware spectral feature extraction, we bridged the gap between raw instrumental data and machine learning frameworks. Our LightGBM-DART implementation, Spec2Prop-Edge, effectively categorizes nine inorganic chemical families and predicts essential material properties with strong macro-F1 performance. Spec2Prop-Edge is intended as a confidence-aware screening assistant for candidate prioritization and preliminary material-family interpretation. It does not replace expert spectroscopic interpretation, crystallographic validation, or laboratory confirmation. We hope this open benchmark accelerates the development of robust, multimodal AI models in materials science.
